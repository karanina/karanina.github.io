At Piha Holiday Lodges we offer affordable and comfortable beachside accomodation.

We're located directly across the road from South Piha Beach, so whether you're wanting family fun in the sand, to surf the breaks, or to explore the stunning Waitakere Ranges, our lodges are a great place to establish a base for your holiday.

Our lodges are of varying sizes, accomodating between 1 and 8 guests. Each lodge has it's own en suite and mini fridge.

Guests are welcome to use the communal kitchen facilities in the main building, as well as relax in the Guest Lounge. We also have surf boards and other beach equipment available for rental if required.

So come join us at Piha Holiday Lodges for your West Coast adventure!
