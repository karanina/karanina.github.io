Welcome to Piha Beach Lodges, luxury hostel accomodation at Auckland's wild and scenic Piha Beach.

Nestled in the foothills of the Waitakere Ranges, our lodges are less than 1km to Piha Beach.

Our lodges has can accomodate between 4 and 10 guests, and all lodges have their own en suite bathroom facilities.

A central common room provides access to kitchen facilities and recreation equipment available for the use of guests.

Recreation equipment includes:

  • Surfboards
  • Body boards
  • Googles
  • Flippers
  • Table tennis equipment
  • Sand buckets and spades
  • Board games
  • Playing cards

So whether you want to hike in the bush, or surf at the beach, come and join us for your stay.

We'd love to have you.
